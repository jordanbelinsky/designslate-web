Features

Learn more about the factors that go into making an app from DesignSlate. I understand the importance of a quality user experience, and all the factors that are at play to create such an experience.

Newest SDK Features

Making use of the newest available features in the SwiftUI iOS framework, apps are equipped to provide the best native iOS experience possible. Features include native dark mode, homescreen and notification centre widgets, and cross-platform availability (iOS/iPadOS/macOS).

Sleek and Modern Design

Featuring elegant use of white-space and an easy-to-use UI/UX, my app design echos modern simplistic design, giving each app an inviting allure. Each app is designed using responsive design, ensuring the best possible experience across all screen-sizes and aspect ratios.

Security and Responsibility

Security is a priority, with Apple's CoreData databases being used to ensure all data is secured locally on-device, with no information being shared or accessed outside of the user's device. No account is required to fully utilize features within any app, with syncing completed via iCloud.

History of DesignSlate

Learn more about how DesignSlate was started, and the evolution of the brand since its inception.

  • June 2019

    Educ[alcul]ate for iOS is Released

    Based upon an earlier Python-made proof of concept, I created Educ[alcul]ate to enable students to track their progress in school. The app was released as an individual project not associated with a brand.

  • November 2019

    DesignSlate is Created

    I created this brand, titled DesignSlate. This is the new brand name which all of my apps will be created as a part of. Educ[alcul]ate undergoes a rebrand, being included as a DesignSlate application.

  • October 2020

    DesignSlate 2.0

    DesignSlate is given a fresh coat of paint, with a new logo. Work begun on Educ[alcul]ate 2.0, rewritten using the new version of SwiftUI, as well as an unnamed new iOS/macOS project.

About Me


Jordan Belinsky

Founder


My name is Jordan Belinsky. I am a student studying Computer Science at Queen's University in Kingston, ON. I began taking an interest in app development during high school, releasing my first app just before graduating.

I set out to create this brand in order to consolidate any apps I create into a single entity. I feel a good user interface design can make or break any app, and it is the single most important factor to me when developing apps.

My goal is to create a bundle of apps which all feature a streamlined modern interface, ensuring that users come to love the design cues represented in my apps.
